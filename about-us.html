How do we do without brokerage?

Revenue is the core of any business to run and so is ours.

We are very transparent with Tenants and our Partners regarding our policies and model.

In order to add value to our customers, we do not charge anything from Tenants and Property owners. We work hard on making our Rental platform 100% Brokerage-Free.

We verify each and every property and service partner.

We earn money by offering Value-Added services on Rentals and Personalised services to you, in order to keep ourselves working to serve you better.
